Our Aim

The Stutor project aims to provide a social interactive platform for students to connect with other students who can offer them academic help they need. Our goal is to create a positive impact on the varsity community by improving the lives of students who struggle with their grades and are unable to acquire assistance.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading academic assistance platform that connects students by providing them with a reliable and efficient service. We seek to inspire other young minds to come up with solutions to various problems and improve people's lives.

Our Services

Private Tutoring

We offer private tutoring to students who need extra help in a particular subject. Our experienced tutors are available to meet with students one-on-one either online or in-person.
